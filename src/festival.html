BRITISH SUMMER TIME

British Summer Time Hyde Park (BST Hyde Park) is a music festival held over two weekends once a year in London's Hyde Park.

It was the first event AEG Presents held in Hyde Park since they acquired the rights to host concerts there, and features a wide range of musical genres. Since 2013, BST Hyde Park has seen headliners such as Celine Dion, The Rolling Stones, Bon Jovi, Barbra Streisand, Lionel Richie, Kylie Minogue, Black Sabbath, Neil Young, Tom Petty & The Heartbreakers, Arcade Fire, The Who, Blur, The Strokes and Taylor Swift. In 2019 the capacity was 65,000.
